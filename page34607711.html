About us
WHO ARE WE?
A team of like-minded people consisting of:
• Graduates from and coaches of British schools and academies
• Professional players and coaches from England, South Africa, France and
Russia
• Graduates and students of PFUR (Peoples' Friendship University of Russia)
OUR MISSION & CORE VALUES
"Rugby is a hooligans’ game played by gentlemen."
(Winston Churchill)
Building bridges
  1. Creation of a unique club in Russia, where foreign and Russian-speaking children are able to train together.
  2. Creation of a unique community for children, parents, graduates, teachers, sponsors and everyone who is interested.
  3. "Building bridges" between schools in Russia and the rest of the world through joint school projects and educational programs for teaching and playing rugby. Organization of a continuous exchange process of knowledge and experience.
CORE VALUES
1. Mutual respect regardless of gender, race, nationality and social status.
2. Nobility.
3. Team – Family.
